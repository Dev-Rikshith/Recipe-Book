# Bottle Gourd Raita

*A simple and comforting South Indian-style raita with bottle gourd, curd, and South Indian tempering.*

**Servings:** 2

---

## Ingredients

- 1 small bottle gourd
- 1 cup curd
- Salt to taste

### Tempering

- 1 tsp oil
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- 1–2 dried red chillies
- A few curry leaves

### Optional Additions

- Coriander leaves
- 1 green chilli

---

## Method

1. Heat oil in a pan.
2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add cumin seeds, dried red chillies, and curry leaves.
4. Add finely chopped bottle gourd and sauté until soft and cooked.
5. Let the bottle gourd cool slightly.
6. Mix it with curd in a bowl.
7. Add salt to taste and mix well.
8. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
