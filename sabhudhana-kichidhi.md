# Sabudana Khichdi

A light, flavourful dish made from tapioca pearls. The key to perfect khichdi is properly soaked sabudana — fluffy, separate, and non-sticky.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 cup sabudana (tapioca pearls)
- ¾ cup water (for soaking)
- 2 tbsp oil
- 8–10 curry leaves
- 1 medium potato, boiled and diced *(optional)*
- ½ cup roasted peanuts, coarsely crushed
- Salt, to taste
- ½ tsp black pepper, freshly cracked
- 1–2 tsp lemon juice

---

## Instructions

**1. Wash & Soak**
Rinse the sabudana under cold running water, rubbing gently, until the water runs completely clear and all starch is removed. Add ¾ cup water per 1 cup sabudana and soak for 4–6 hours or overnight. Drain before cooking.

**2. Cook the base**
Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add curry leaves and let them splutter for 30 seconds. If using potato, add the boiled diced pieces and cook for 2–3 minutes until lightly golden.

**3. Add the sabudana**
Add the drained sabudana and stir gently. Cook on medium-low heat for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pearls turn translucent.

**4. Season & finish**
Add salt, cracked pepper, and crushed peanuts. Stir to combine, turn off the heat, and squeeze over the lemon juice.

---

Serve hot, on its own or with yogurt or green chutney.
